Pizza Solver

A tool for figuring out what pizzas to order when you're eating with a group.


Everyone sets their topping preferences ahead of time, and when someone starts an order they pick a restaurant, how many pizzas, and who's eating. The app then solves for the best pizza assignments and toppings that work for the people on it.
